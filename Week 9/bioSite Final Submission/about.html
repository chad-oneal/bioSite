Matthew is an expert and a leader in the world of Engineering. Having spent the last 12 years as a Mechanical Engineer and specializing in process improvement with focuses on reducing production time, flow, and ergonomics he has gained a mastery of the skills, the knowledge, and intangibles that can only come from professional experience. He consistently solves problems of inefficiency regarding line production time and improvements. Prior to becoming an Engineer Matthew was a student at Middle Tennessee State University (MTSU). While attending MTSU, Matthew was and still is a very involved family man and a man with a passion for his hobbies. He married his wife Deanna in the spring of 2004 and they have two children. As Matthew thrives on challenging himself to continue to grow and be a better person than he was the day before; his accomplishments shine in both his professional and personal life. Being an excellent father is paramount in his personal life. He believes that even though we live in an unfair world, that it is important to teach his children to deal with this world with emotional intelligence and above all Love. Matthew is also quite an artist which serves him both professionally and personally.
